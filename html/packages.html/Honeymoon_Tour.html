Honeymoon Tour in Nepal

TRIP HIGHLIGHTS

Honeymoon tour in Nepal is a romantic tour package for newly wedded couples. The couples can have their private and intimate moment with their love in the beautiful Himalayan country. The culturally rich country, Nepal can be the best honeymoon location for the traditional holiday of newly married couple. The sweetest moment of married life can be experienced in the fascinating locations of Nepal. The tour of nature, culture, landscape, jungle, river, lake and panoramic mountain can be the memorable honeymoon experience for the newly married lovers.

Sightseeing of Kathmandu (city of temples), fun activities at Chitwan national park (rich in wildlife, birds and Tharu culture) and colorful times at Pokhara (city of Lakes) are the romantic activities that our tour package includes during Honeymoon tour in Nepal. The tour around the UNESCO World Heritage Site listed sites such as Bouddhanath stupa, Swayambhunath, Pashupatinath temple and Kathmandu Durbar Square would be the initial introduction about Nepal. The wedded couples can enjoy the Tharu dance and cultural program after their arrival at Chitwan national park. Chitwan national park is rich in green beauty, wildlife and is home for more than 600 species of bird. Different activities like canoe ride at the peaceful Rapti River, elephant back safari into the jungle, bathing with elephant and romantic view of sunset from the bank of river are pleasurable experience for the nature loving couples.

The next destination is the beautiful city Pokhara. Pokhara is also called the city of lakes. During a clear weather, a lake amidst the city reflects the beautiful Himalayas offering a pleasant sight. The view of sunrise and mountain ranges like Annapurna, Dhaulagiri, Machhapuchhre etc from the hill of Sarankot is heavenly. The evening walk around the beautiful Fewa Lake with your loved one is a beautiful experience. The sightseeing tour around the beautiful city of Pokhara is an amazing experience. The sunset and sunrise view from our next destination Nagarkot is alluring. Honeymoon tour package provided by Lovely Nepal Tours and Travel is a beautiful package that consists of natural and cultural beauties of Nepal. Lovely Nepal Tours and Travel is determined to provide you with beautiful memories that would last lifetime. Our team also has expertise in making a customized tour packages according to your preferences.

Day 01

Arrival at Kathmandu.

Upon your arrival at the International airport at Kathmandu, representative of MMAT NEPAL will receive, welcome and transfer you to a hotel. You are given a short briefing about the next day activities. Stay overnight at the hotel.

Day 02

Sightseeing and cultural tour of UNESCO World Heritage Sites in Kathmandu.

After the breakfast, you are guided out for city sightseeing trip. The tour guide would escort you to different UNESCO World Heritage Sites like Swayambhunath, Kathmandu Durbar square, Bouddhanath and Pashupati temple. After watching the aarati and cremation ceremony on the bank of river at Pashupatinath, drive back to the hotel and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 03

Drive to Chitwan national park.

In the morning, we drive towards Chitwan national park. After 5-6 hours of scenic drive through beautiful forest, hills, rivers and waterfalls we arrive at Chitwan national park and check in at a hotel. After couple of hours of rest, we walk around the local Tharu village and enjoy the beautiful sight of sunset from the bank of Rapti River. Return back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 04

Fun activities at Chitwan National Park.

Chitwan National Park is rich in green beauty, wildlife and bird species. It is home for the rare one-horned rhinoceros and the mighty Bengal tiger. Whole day we keep ourselves busy in different jungle activities like romantic Canoe ride, Elephant back safari, bathing with elephant and enjoy Tharu cultural dance at hotel and stay overnight.

Day 05

Drive to Pokhara ‘The city of lakes'.

After breakfast at the hotel we drive to Pokhara through rivers, jungles and hills. After 5-6 hours of scenic drive we arrive at pokhara and move into a hotel. After few hours of private time at the hotel we get out for romantic boating around the peaceful Fewa Lake and visit Barahi Temple. We walk back towards the hotel for overnight stay.

Day 06

Sunrise view from Sarangkot and full day tour of Pokhara.

Early morning we drive towards Sarangkot hill. Sarangkot offers the most beautiful view of sunrise and Himalayas like Annapurna, Machhapuchhre and Dhaulagiri range. We return back to Pokhara and enjoy the sightseeing tour of beautiful locations like David falls, Seti gorge, Mahendra cave and many others and stay overnight at the hotel.

Day 07

Drive to Nagarkot from Pokhara.

Nagarkot is a popular view point and famous tourist destination. In the morning we drive towards Nagarkot from Pokhara. After 6-7 hours of scenic drive we reach Nagarkot. Nagarkot offers a beautiful sight of sunset and Himalayan ranges surrounding Kathmandu valley. Spend some quality time and stay overnight at the hotel.

Day 08

Departure.

Thank you for your trip with MMAT NEPAL. Our representative would drop you to the international airport for the final departure to the future destination.
