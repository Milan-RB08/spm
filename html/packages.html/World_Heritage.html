World Heritage Tour in Nepal

TRIP HIGHLIGHTS

TRIP HIGHLIGHTS

  • Sightseeing tours of UNESCO World Heritage Sites in Kathmandu (City of temples), Pashupatinath  temple (Hindu Shrine), Boudhnath (Buddhist Stupa), Kathmandu Durbar Square, Swayambunath Stupa (Monkey Temple). Patan Durbar Square and Bhakatapur Durbar Square.
  • Amazing Mountain flight.
  • Scenic drive from Kathmandu-Chitwan- Lumbini-Kathmandu.
  • Amazing view of Gauri Shankar (7146m), Dorje Lakpa (6975m), Langtang Lirung (7246m) and Shisha Pangma (8027m) from Bhakatapur.
  • Memorial activities at Chitwan National Park (UNESCO Heritage Site) including Elephant back safari, Canoe ride, Jungle walk, Birds watching, Tharu culture dance, Village walk, Bath with Elephant and visit Elephant Breeding Centre.
  • Full day sightseeing Lord Buddha birth place in Lumbini. 

This World Heritage sites Tours in Nepal is one of the best tour package among the Nepal traveler. The heritage tour is specially designed to see and explore all the UNESCO World Heritage sites in Nepal. The heritage tour cover most of Nepal's UNESCO world heritage sites in Kathmandu, Bhaktapur, Patan, Chitwan National Park, Sagarmatha National Park, Lumbini Birth place of Lord Buddha and Pokhara. While making a list of world heritage, the heritages are selected and examined on the criteria of their historical, natural and cultural significance, their artistic value, their rare quality and their unique characteristic which identify the nation. United Nations Educational Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) have declared Ten World Heritage Sites in Nepal among them seven sites are inside Kathmandu Valley and three sites are outside of Kathmandu Valley.
Out of ten World Heritage Sites in Nepal eight sites are cultural heritage sites and others two are natural heritage sites having outstanding universal value. Seven out of eight cultural sites are located inside the Kathmandu valley such as Kathmandu Durbar Square- The house of the Living Goddess, the ferocious Kal Bhairab, the red monkey god, and hundreds of erotic carvings are a few examples of the sights at the Square, Patan Durbar Square- The square boasts of many famous sites and unique architecture, Bhaktapur Durbar- is a conglomeration of pagoda and shikhara-style temples grouped around a fifty-five window palace of brick and wood. The square is one of the most charming architectural showpieces of the Valley as it highlights the ancient arts of Nepal. Square, the Swayambhunath"the self-existent"- is a major landmark of the Valley, Bauddhanath- Bouddhanath is among the largest stupas in South Asia, and it has become the focal point of Tibetan Buddhism in Nepal,
Pashupatinath- is the holiest Hindu pilgrimage destination in Nepal. There are linga images of Shiva along with statues, shrines, and temples dedicated to other deities in the complex. And Changu Narayan- is the preserver of creation to Hindus. His temple near Changu village is often described as the most ancient temple in the Kathmandu Valley are consisting within the Kathmandu Valley and left one as Lumbini ( the birth place of Lord Buddha) ,Chitwan national Park and Sagarmatha National Park Natural World Heritage enlisted by the UNESCO. Chitwan National Park is full of dense forest and house of the more than 450 species of birds, royal Bengal tiger, one horned Rhino, Gharil Crocodile, Asian elephant and sloth bear. In general Chitwan National park is very rich in flora and fauna. Sagarmatha national park is the Home of the world’s highest Mountain Mount Everest as well as hundreds of peaks. This is one of the best trekking trails in the Nepal. Each year thousands of trekkers visit the foot of the Mount Everest.
Nepal is one of the most fascinating countries in the world with its natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, towering Pagodas, spectacular Himalayan Ranges with world’s highest peaks. It is a country of ethnic and topographical diversities, rich flora & fauna, adorn with exceptional Architecture unmatched by any country of the world. You will gain invaluable and incomparable insights into the diverse ethnic groups, - their lifestyles, social behavioral patterns, traditions, religion and beliefs.

Day 01 Arrive in Kathmandu international airport (1345meters).

Overnight at hotel.Welcome to the Himalayan country of Nepal. Representative of Lovely Nepal Tours and Travels receives at the airport and transfer you to the hotel. A short briefing is given to you about the activities of tomorrow. Overnight at hotel.

 

Day 02 Everest Mountain Flight and sightseeing around Kathmandu valley.

We take an exciting Everest mountain flight in the morning. It is a magnificent opportunity to view the highest peak of the world from a closer distance. We guarantee window seat for this spectacular trip. After landing at Kathmandu, we take to the city sightseeing. Kathmandu is a city of temples. The tour escorts you to the UNESCO listed World Heritage Sites. Temples and Buddhist Stupas are the focused travelling monumental sites. Pashupatinath (Hindu shrine), Boudhanath (Buddhist Stupa), Kathmandu Durbar Square and Swayambhunath Stupa are some of them. The mediaeval arts and architectures are amazing. Drive back to the hotel and take a rest. Overnight at hotel. (B&B)

 

Day 03 City tour of Bhaktapur Durbar and Patan Durbar square.

Bhaktapur is traditionally known as Bhad Gaon. It is one of the cities in the Kathmandu valley that is full of temples and sculptures. Bhaktapur Durbar Square is the central attraction of this Newari town. Different pagoda style temples, stone and wooden sculptures, 55 windows palace, golden gate, museum, pottery square and others are significant. Patan Durbar Square is unique on its infrastructure. The temple is dedicated to Lord Krishna. The city itself is known for its fine arts. Then back to hotel and rest. Overnight at hotel.

 

Day 04 Drive from Kathmandu to Chitwan.

About 6 hours.In the morning, we drive to the Chitwan National Park to enjoy the Jungle safari in Nepal. It is the most visited national park of Nepal. The road drive is picturesque. The green hills, forests, waterfalls and Rivers are awesome. We reach Chitwan in about 6 hours and check in at the hotel. Overnight at hotel.

 

Day 05 Full day Chitwan jungle activities at Chitwan National Park

After the breakfast, our jungle activities begin. The whole day we keep ourselves busy in Elephant back safari, bird watching, jungle walk, jeep safari, canoe ride and others. Chitwan National Park is a home of one-horned rhino and the Bengal tiger, including several species of flora and fauna. We also walk through the Tharu villages. They are the indigenous group of Nepal. Return back to hotel and rest. Overnight at hotel.

 

Day 06 Drive to Lumbini from Chitwan.

After breakfast, we drive to Lumbini from Chitwan. The Highway is straight and flat, so the driving is awesome. The sceneries of the green fields and commercial hubs can be seen. Lumbini is the birthplace of Lord Buddha, who spread the message of peace to all over the world. Walk around the Tharu village in the evening. Overnight at hotel.

 

Day 07 Full days sightseeing around Lumbini.

We go on exploring the Lumbini heritage site. Various features have made this place eye- catching destination in the world. Maya Devi temple, Puskarini Pool, garden, monasteries of different countries, museum and other attractions are spectacular. Pilgrimage trip of Buddhist devotees from different countries come to pay their homage. The surrounding is populated by the indigenous Tharu communities, where you can visit and know the unique lifestyle. Overnight at hotel.

 

Day 08 Drive back to Kathmandu.

If you want to fly, then drive to Bhairahawa and fly back to Kathmandu. Otherwise, In the morning we drive back to Kathmandu through the Prithvi Highway. Arriving Kathmandu, transfer to the hotel and rest. Overnight at hotel.

 

Day 09 Buffer day in Kathmandu.

This special day is for your purpose. You can buy souvenirs and other things around the local market of Thamel or near your hotel. Then enjoy the last moment of your interests. Overnight at hotel.

 

Day 10 Departure for your onward destination.

Thank you for your trip with Lovely Nepal Tours and Travels. Our representative drops to the airport for the final departure or to your further destination.
