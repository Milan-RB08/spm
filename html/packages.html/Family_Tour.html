Family Tour in Nepal

TRIP HIGHLIGHTS

TRIP HIGHLIGHTS

  • Sightseeing tour of UNESCO World Heritage Sites in Kathmandu, Pasupatinath (Hindu Shrine), Boudhnath (Buddhist Stupa),Kathmandu Durbar Square and Swayambunath Stupa.
  • Full day Jungle activities at Chitwan National Park (Hearth of Jungle) including Elephant back safari, Bath with Elephant, Birds Watching, Canoe Ride and Jungle walk.
  • Scenic drive from Kathmandu- Chitwan- Lumbini- Palpa- Pokhara- Kathmandu.
  • Full day sightseeing Lord Buddha Birth place in Lumbini.
  • Visit Historical place Palpa Tansen.
  • Visit city of alluring of Pokhara.

MMAT Nepal Travel offers a reasonable option for family holidays to all adventure travelers. This tour is designed to explore the mysterious culture and tradition of Nepal which is appropriate to travel with family. Especially this tour is dedicated to those travelers who travel with children, which is the best combination of easy walking and sightseeing around the popular cities of Nepal with your family. Nepal is one of the best destinations in the word to make a holiday with children, family can have fun together during tour period and will get an opportunity to explore Nepal with your family and know about the Nepalese peoples, lifestyle of children and natural beauty of Nepal. It is one of the most memorable and delightful experience can be made with family members. Nepal is synonymous with its mighty Himalaya ranges, ever smiling hospitable and friendly people, rich flora and fauna, unique culture and tradition. Due to the diversity in Nepal’s nature and exotic culture makes this country ideal for tour and travel activities with family and friends.
In This family tour in Nepal package you will get chance to visit the Heritage sites of Kathmandu valley including Buddhist Stupas, Hindu Temples and Old Durbar Squares or Royal Palaces. United Nations Educational Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) have declared Ten World Heritage Sites in Nepal among them seven sites are inside Kathmandu Valley & Three sites are outside of Kathmandu Valley. Kathmandu valley is culturally very rich which one of the spiritual valley in Nepal, as many Hindu and Buddhist gods and goddesses, Sages and Gurus (teachers) visited this place in the ancient time. People from diverse cultural, ethnic, and religious background lives in Kathmandu valley where the arts, architects, festivals, and ritual are quite diverse. The 'Newars' is the original inhabitant of Kathmandu valley. Pokhara is the paradise city and is filled with natural beauty and cultural places. Pokhara can be taken as the second most popular destination in this country, offering lakeside leisure with astounding views of the majestic Himalayas. We also explore famous Phewa lake, caves, Davis falls and many other sacred temples and stupas. An exciting sightseeing tour will let you experience some of its secrets.
Chitwan National Park is famous for the diverse natural environment and wide variety of animals and birds where you will have an action packed jungle adventure, including an elephant back safari and bird-watching tour. A day long jungle safari on an elephant's back concludes superbly with a cultural program performed by local Tharu villages. Back again to Kathmandu, very morning mountain flight provides outstanding views of Mt Everest towering high above the other high Himalayas. There is no doubt that family tour in Nepal provides a wonderful opportunity for engaging with local people and family. So have a very full fun loving and exciting tour with your family in Nepal making your every moment most amazing with happiness and experience long remembered by the whole family. This tour also an educational tour for your kids. As you will visit from low plain level to highest mountain range viewpoints. You will see lots of bio-diversity. You will get a chance to view hundreds of kinds of birds, animals, trees.

DETAIL ITINERARY

Day 01

Arrive at Kathmandu airport (1345 meters). Welcome to the Himalayan country of Nepal. Representative of MMAT Nepal Tours and Travels receives at the airport and transfer you to the hotel. A short briefing is given to you about the activities of tomorrow. Overnight at hotel. (B&B)

Day 02

Everest Mountain flight and Full day sightseeing around Kathmandu valley.We take an exciting Everest mountain flight in the morning. It is a magnificent opportunity to view the highest peak of the world from a closer distance. We guarantee window seat for this spectacular trip. After landing at Kathmandu, we take to the city sightseeing. Kathmandu is a city of temples. The tour escorts you to the UNESCO listed World Heritage Sites. Temples and Buddhist Stupas are the focused travelling monumental sites. Pashupatinath (Hindu shrine), Boudhanath (Buddhist Stupa), Kathmandu Durbar Square and Swayambhunath Stupa are some of them. The mediaeval arts and architectures are amazing. Drive back to the hotel and take a rest. Overnight at hotel. (B&B)

Day 03

Drive to Chitwan from Kathmandu. About 5-6 hours.In the morning, we drive to the Chitwan National Park to enjoy the Jungle safari in Nepal. It is the most visited national park of Nepal. The road drive is picturesque. The green hills, forests, waterfalls and Rivers are awesome. We reach Chitwan in about 5 hours and check in at the hotel. overnight at hotel. (B, L, D)

Day 04

Full day Jungle activities at Chitwan National Park. After the breakfast, our jungle activities begin. The whole day we keep ourselves busy in Elephant back safari, bird watching, jungle walk, canoe ride and others. Chitwan National Park is a home of one-horned rhino and the Bengal tiger, including several species of for and fauna. We also walk through the Tharu villages. They are the indigenous group of Nepal. Return back to hotel and rest. Overnight at hotel. (B, L, D)

Day 05

Drive from Chitwan to Lumbini. About 5 hours.After breakfast, we drive to Lumbini from Chitwan. The Highway is straight and flat, so the driving is awesome. The sceneries of the green fields and commercial hubs can be seen. Lumbini is the birthplace of Lord Buddha, who spread the message of peace to all over the world. Walk around the Tharu village in the evening. Overnight at hotel. (B & B)

Day 06

Full day sightseeing tour around Lumbini.We go on exploring the Lumbini heritage site. Various features have made this place eye-catching destination in the world. Maya Devi temple, Puskarini Pool, garden, monasteries of different countries, museum and other attractions are spectacular. Pilgrimage trip of Buddhist devotees from different countries come to pay their homage. The surrounding is populated by the indigenous Tharu communities, where you can visit and know the unique lifestyle. Overnight at hotel. (B & B)

Day 07

Drive from Lumbini to Palpa Tansen.After breakfast, we drive to Palpla, a historical town in the western Nepal. It is mostly populated by the Newar and Magar communities. Sitalpati Narayan templ and Tansen Shreenagar are some wonderful places. The biggest wooden door of Nepal, Bhagwati Than, Bhairab Than and other attractions are exciting to explore. The Pine forests look to be a trimmed forest. Overnight at lodge. (B & B)

Day 08

Drive from Palpa Tansen to Pokhara. Overnight at hotel.In the morning, we drive to Pokhara, which is the city of lakes. The winding road above the River, forests, villages, fields all are awesome. Reaching Pokhara, transfer to the hotel. If you like, we can get out for the boating or walk around the city. Overnight at hotel. (B & B)

Day 09

Full day sightseeing tour around Pokhara.We drive to Sarangkot to enjoy the early morning sunrise view over the Annapurna region White Mountains. They include Mt. Annapurna (8091m), Dhaulagiri (8163m), Fishtail, and Himchuli and others. You can enjoy the paragliding because Sarangkot is one of the best paragliding spots in the world. Returning back to Pokhara, city sightseeing can be done as the further program. David Falls, Seto gorge, Mahendra cave, peace stupas are some of the popular sites. Overnight at hotel. (B & B)

Day 10

Drive from Pokhara to Kathmandu. About 5-6 hours.In the morning we drive back to Kathmandu through the Prithvi Highway. Arriving Kathmandu, transfer to the hotel and rest. Overnight at hotel. (B & B)

Day 11

Transfer to international airport for your final distination.Thank you for your trip with Lovely Nepal Tours and Travels. Our representative drops to the airport for the final departure or to your further destination.
