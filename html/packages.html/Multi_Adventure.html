Multi Adventure Tour in Nepal

TRIP HIGHLIGHTS

  • Circumnavigating on a mountain flight to observe the beautiful Himalayas of Nepal including the world’s tallest peak, Mount Everest.
  • Sightseeing and cultural tour of UNESCO World Heritage Sites in Kathmandu like Bouddhanath (Buddhist Stupa), Swayambhunath and Pashupatinath (Hindu Shrine).
  • Jungle activities at Chitwan National Park (UNESCO World Heritage listed site) such as Elephant Back Safari, Bath with elephant, Canoeing, Jungle walk, Bird watching, Attending Tharu cultural program and Beautiful sunset view from bank of river.
  • Amazing paragliding experience, sharing the airspace with the eagle, vulture, kites and birds on the sky of the beautiful city Pokhara.
  • Panoramic view of different mountain ranges from Sarangkot and Nagarkot.
  • Alluring view of sunset and sunrise from Chitwan, Sarangkot and Nagarkot.
  • Thrilling rafting experience at the steepest river in Nepal.
  • Bungee jumping from 166m long suspension bridge into the fast flowing river at Sindhupalchok.

“Life is either a daring adventure or nothing”:- Helen Keller

If you wish to perform recreational and adventurous fun activities rather than just an ordinary vacation in Nepal, Multi adventure tour package offered by Lovely Nepal Tours and Travel is the best package for you. Multi adventure tour package includes major extreme sports at most amazing locations of Nepal. The tour package takes you out of your comfort zone and provides you with the best travel experience. All the components of the adventure travel i.e. physical activities, cultural exchange and nature connection is included in this multi adventure tour package.

The adventure tour includes many exciting sport and adventure activities like mountain flight, paragliding, elephant back safari, bathing with elephant, canoeing, rafting, cultural sightseeing tour and bungee jumping. Mountain flight offers the magnificent bird’s eye view of the mountain ranges in Nepal including the highest peak in the world, Mount Everest. Sightseeing tour at Kathmandu ‘the city of temple’ gives the opportunity to know about the ancient Nepalese culture and tradition. Sightseeing tour around the UNESCO World Heritage sites like Bouddhanath, Swayambhunath and Pashupatinath is the beautiful cultural experience. Various jungle activities like Elephant back safari, Bath with elephant, Canoeing, Exploring the wildlife and birds etc takes us close to the wildness of nature. Paragliding at Pokhara (the city of lakes) provides the most beautiful sight of lake, valley, jungle, and nature surrounded by the massive Himalayan ranges like Annapurna, Machhapuchhre, Himchuli and Dhaulagiri range.

Nagarkot offers the beautiful view of sunset and sunrise along with the panoramic view of mountain ranges surrounding the valley. Bungee jumping experience from 166m long suspension bridge above the fast flowing white water is the nerve breaking experience. Rafting at the steepest river with multiple rapids excites the brain and induces the adrenaline into the blood providing the best experience for the adventure lovers. Multi adventure tour offers the opportunity to explore the nature, culture and discover your own perceptive of experience by performing these thrilling activities. 

 

detail itinery

Day 01

Arrival at Kathmandu.

Representative of Lovely Nepal Tours and Travel will receive you from airport and transfer you to a hotel upon your arrival at the International airport. A short briefing about the next day activities is given and you’ll stay overnight at the hotel. (B &B)

Day 02

Mountain flight and Kathmandu sightseeing tour.

Early morning you are driven to the domestic airport terminal and given briefing about the mountain flight experience. Observation of the beautiful Himalayan range including the highest mountain, Mt Everest from the mountain flight. After circumnavigating the Himalayas, we return back and prepare for sightseeing tour of Kathmandu. We observe beautiful UNESCO listed cultural heritage sites like Swayambhunath, Bouddhanath and cremation ceremony at Pashupatinath temple. After the tour we return back to the hotel and stay overnight. (B&B)

Day 03

Drive to Chitwan National Park.

Early in the morning we start our 5-6 hour drive towards Chitwan. On arrival at Chitwan we will have lunch and rest for couple of hours. Your tour guide will then assist you to the walk through Tharu village. After seeing the mesmerizing view of sunset from the bank of river you are assisted back to hotel. Then you will observe the Tharu cultural dance and have your dinner. (B,L,D)

Day 04

Jungle activities at Chitwan National Park.

After the breakfast, our jungle activity begins. We will start our jungle adventure with elephant back safari and bath with elephant followed by canoeing, jungle walk and bird watching. Chitwan National Park is home for One- horned rhino and Bengal tiger, including several species of flora and fauna. Canoeing along the Rapti River provide the opportunity to spot Gharial and Marsh Mugger crocodile on their natural habitat. (B,L,D)

Day 05

Drive to Pokhara (the city of lakes).

After breakfast at the hotel we drive to Pokhara through rivers, jungles and hills. After 5-6 hours of scenic drive we arrive at pokhara and move into a hotel. After few hours of rest at the hotel we get out for walk around the peaceful Fewa Lake. We walk back towards the hotel for overnight stay. (B&B)

Day 06

Paragliding from Sarangkot hill.

Early morning we drive towards Sarangkot hill. On a clear day, Sarangkot offers the most beautiful view Himalayas like Annapurna, Machhapuchhre, Himchuli and Dhaulagiri range. After the short briefing about the paragliding, fly over the beautiful city of Pokhara with bird’s eye view of dense forest, valley, Himalaya and Fewa Lake. After the exciting flight our representative will pick you up from the landing spot at Lakeside and drive back to the hotel. We return back to hotel and enjoy the sightseeing tour of beautiful locations like David falls, Seti gorge, Mahendra cave and many others and stay overnight at the hotel. (B&B)

Day 07

Drive to Nagarkot from Pokhara.

Nagarkot is a popular view point and famous tourist destination. In the morning we drive towards Nagarkot from Pokhara. After 6-7 hours of scenic drive we reach Nagarkot. Nagarkot offers a beautiful sight of sunset and Himalayan ranges surrounding Kathmandu valley. Stay overnight at the hotel. (B&B)

Day 08

Drive to Kodari and enjoy Bungee jump.

After 3 hours of drive we reach the bungee jumping spot at Sindhupalchok. Briefing and equipping for the bungee jump. After the thrilling bungee jump from 166m high suspension bridge we move towards the rafting location at Bhotekoshi River. We enjoy at the beach, enjoy the buffet dinner and stay overnight in a tented camp.

Day 09

Rafting at Bhotekoshi River and back to Kathmandu.

Bhotekoshi River is the steepest river for rafting in Nepal. After the most thrilling rafting experience at the Bhotekoshi River we reach Dolalghat beach and end our rafting. We drive back towards Kathmandu and reach Kathmandu after couple of hours of drive. We rest at a hotel and stay overnight. (B&B)

Day 10

Sightseeing tour at Patan and Bhaktapur and Farewell Dinner.

After many days of tiring adventure activities we perform a sightseeing tour around the city of fine arts (Patan) and city of devotees (Bhaktapur) as a part of cultural tour. We can also do shopping for souvenir. We can see traditional Nepali Dance at a restaurant during the farewell dinner offered by Lovely Nepal Tours and Travel. We walk around the market of Kathmandu valley and return to hotel for packing and overnight stay.

Day 11

Departure.

Thank you for the Multi adventure tour in Nepal with MMAT NEPAL. Our representative will drop you to the airport for your final departure to the future destination.
